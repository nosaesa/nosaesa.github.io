signals & systems

Here are some nice materials regarding signals & systems:

books

While Oppenheim's classic text on signals & systems is a great reference for those who are already familiar with the material, it's pretty hard to use for learning. Whether you're new to the subject or just interested in seeing the material through a different lens, I highly recommend the following texts which develop signals & systems using geometry of vector spaces:

  • Introduction to Orthogonal Transforms, by Ruye Wang

  • Foundations of Signal Processing, by Martin Vetterli, Jelena Kovacevic, and Vivek Goyal
